On Thu, Jan 04, 2007 at 09:41:08AM +0100, Steve Schnepp wrote:
> Actually, now I'm more asking myself if jigdo would still be a major
> debian distribution channel, since this mailing-list is quite quiet.
> [ Either there are not much bugs or not much users :-p ]

:) I guess the fact that Matthias offers HTTP and rsync access to the 
weekly Debian images means that less people use jigdo. Still, I don't think 
it's dead, not even remotely - many people use it because it is very 
bandwidth-efficient. Also, many people with jigdo problems post to 
debian-cd.

A lot could still be done with the idea. For example, a server-side CGI 
version of jigdo could assemble images on the fly, so by installing it, a 
regular Debian package mirror could serve the CD images without additional 
disk space requirements. However, I have less time for jigdo these days...
